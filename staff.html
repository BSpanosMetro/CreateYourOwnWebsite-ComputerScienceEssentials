Meet Our Team

Alb

Founder & CEO

Albert, who we fondly refer to as Alb, has been with us for many years after forming this establishment. After the passing of his dear friend and debate partner Erwin, he formed this daycare in his memory and that of his wonderful thought experiment where he placed a cat in a box. Now approaching his 145th birthday, we look forward to many more years of his dedication to this organization.

Marie has been with the daycare for almost as long as Alb has. Nearing the age of 157, we can safely say that we have enjoyed her aid in our persuits of cat care.
